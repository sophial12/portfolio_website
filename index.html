About Me

Artist statement & background

I'm Sophia Liu, a multidisciplinary visual artist focusing on thoughtful compositions, muted palettes, and storytelling through subtle details. My practice spans branded illustration systems, editorial layouts, and tactile mixed-media explorations. Each piece reflects a balance between structured professionalism and intuitive play.

With a foundation in design strategy, I collaborate with clients to translate ideas into cohesive visual narratives. Outside of commissioned work, I experiment with personal series that investigate memory, texture, and form.
