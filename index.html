About Me

Artist statement & background

My name is Sophia Liu, a sophomore at the University of Michigan majoring in Economics and minoring in UX Design. I am a multidisciplinary visual artist who focuses on thoughtful compositions and storytelling through details. My passion for design sparked at a young age during a period of illness, when drawing with crayons and markers became both a comfort and a creative escape. Since then, I have used art as both a form of healing and a means of expression, exploring various mediums and creating projects that convey my thoughts and emotions. My practice spans branded illustrations, sculptures, and tactile mixed-media explorations, and I've recently learned to expand my skills into digital art and graphic design.

Grounded in design strategy, I collaborate with clients to transform ideas into cohesive visual narratives, striking a balance between structured professionalism and intuitive creativity in every piece.
